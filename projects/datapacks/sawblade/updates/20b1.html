Changes:

  • Is now designed for Minecraft 1.20.2!
  • This update introduces a lot of new recipes for the Stonecutter.
    • Most of these extends from the Partial Cutback rule as it was largely underdeveloped before.
  • Cutting Bowls now give you only 1 Stick (previously 6).
    • Originally I thought you'd get a Bowl from 3 Planks, but this isn't really the case.

New Recipes:

  • Pressure Plates and Buttons can now be cut back.
    • Previously I thought this wouldn't make sense, but Signs can be cut back.
  • Activator, Detector and Powered Rail can now be cut back into Ingots.
    • Rail can also be cut into 3 Iron Nuggets. (technically 54/16, but the manager takes whole numbers)
  • Blast Furnace and Smoker can now be cutback.
  • Enchanted Books and Books now share the same recipes.
  • Minecarts and their variants (except TNT Minecarts) can now be cutback.
  • Clocks and Compasses can now be cutback.
    • Cutting a Recovery Compass, however, will only give you Echo Shards.
  • Repeaters, Comparators, Observers, Pistons, Dispensers and Droppers can now be cutback.
  • Redstone Lamp and Torch can now be cutback.
  • Hoppers can now be cutback.
    • While this is a potential container, it's not often used as one, so I'll make an exception here.
  • Iron Doors and Trapdoors can now be cut into Ingots.
  • Shield can now be cutback. You can only get 8 Iron Nuggets from this though.
  • Like Lanterns, Soul Lanterns can now be cutback as well.
  • Spectral Arrows can now be cut into Glowstone Dust.
  • Tripwire Hooks can now be cutback into 4 Iron Nuggets, or a Stick.
    • Technically it's 9/2 and 3/2 respectively, but manager takes whole numbers.
    • As a result of this change, cutting Crossbows have also been changed.

Because of my A custom-made tool that creates all possible recipes from the given rules.Relationship Manager, these got expanded into 67 new recipes, for a total of 686!

Partial Cutback details:

  • Partial Cutback returns some of your ingredients back for cutting a crafted result.
    • The crafted item must be from a Crafting Table, and can't be tools or armor.
    • The crafted item must also be from Vanilla, or from my other packs.
    • The cutback must also make sense and wouldn't change Vanilla's design.
  • Special notes:
    • Recipes using especially rare items (Smithing Templates, Beacons, ...) can't be cutback.
    • If a crafting result and its ingredients have different obtaining methods, they can't be cutback.
    • Either way you slice it, edible items can't be made with the Sawblade.
    • Items using multiple craftable options can only be cut into what they all can make.
    • Andesite/Diorite/Granite can't be cutback to get a different block of that set.
    • Walls can't be cut to make the full Block version.
    • Containers (except Hoppers) can't be cut into materials because you'll always lose what's inside.
    • Scaffolding can't be used to cutback Bamboo - those 2 have entirely different properties.
    • Quartz Blocks can't make Quartz due to how different these 2 are in the way you get them.
