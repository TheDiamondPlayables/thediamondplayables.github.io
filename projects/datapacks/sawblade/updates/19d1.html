Important changes:

  • Is now designed for Minecraft 1.19.4 and higher.
    • It's not advised to run this in older versions, I won't be able to provide much support if you do.
      (Imagine having to do that for all older versions for all my datapacks, you'll see a scale issue)
  • Fixed a bug that caused player owned pets (parrots,cats,wolves) to not show their Death Message to their owners properly if killed by a Stonecutter.

Gameplay changes:

  • Damage dealt by the Stonecutter to players now always scales with difficulty.
  • Driving a Minecart with TNT into a Sawblade now triggers an explosion when the Minecart is destroyed.
  • Minecart with Furnace is no longer immune to Sawblade Damage.
  • Sandcrusher now accepts Red Sand related crafts.
  • Mud Bricks can now make Packed Mud.
  • Villagers now take reduced damage from Stonecutters.
    • Rules need to be consistent for all living entities, but Villagers have been working with an open sawblade for ages. Sometimes accidents happen.
  • Villagers now try to hop out of the way if they are damaged by a Sawblade.
    • This should result in some pretty funny interactions whenever you're looking around the world.
    • Shoutout to Kiroto50 on PMC for calling out what I called the Mason Stonecutter issue.
      Completely skipped past this problem when I first developed it. Whoops!
      (I didn't use their suggestion though, which was simpler, but wouldn't be as fun)
