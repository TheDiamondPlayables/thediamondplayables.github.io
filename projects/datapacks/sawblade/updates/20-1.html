Changes:

  • Added 62 new recipes for 1.20 items!
  • Optimized the Damage algorithm with 1.20 stuff!
  • Fixed a bug that stops Damage from dealing damage to entities in a team without friendly fire.

New Recipes Recap:

  • Hanging Signs can be cut into either Stripped Logs or Iron Nuggets.
  • Cherry Wood and Bamboo Blocks acts the same as every other wood.
  • Special cases for Bamboo Blocks though:
    • Bamboo Block = 2 Planks instead of 4.
    • Bamboo Mosaic can be swapped with their non-Mosaic variant in the Stonecutter.
    • Bamboo Blocks can't be cut into Bamboo Fence Gate.
    • Bamboo Blocks can be cut back into Bamboo, but not Stripped Bamboo Blocks.
  • Chiseled Bookshelves can now be cut into Sticks.
  • Calibrated Sculk Sensor can either be cut into 3 Amethyst Shards or the Sculk Sensor.
  • Mangrove Roots can now be cut into a stick.
    • Mojang classified this as being wood, but it's not planks worthy, so 1 stick.

Because of the chain rule, these rules expanded into 62 new recipes.
