Dried Ghasts can't be cut back to prevent it from becoming an alternate source to Ghast Tears.
This is similar to why Quartz Blocks can't be cut back into Nether Quartz - you can also get Quartz Blocks in the Overworld via Trading.

In 1.21.6, Mojang made a sweeping change which makes clickable texts significantly more annoying by adding a forced confirmation screen behind each and every one of them. Best I can think of is that it at least makes it harder for server owners to harm their servers by interacting with arbitrary click actions.There's a good reason as to why they've done this, but the side effect of that change will affect your experience with Admin Books moving forward.
