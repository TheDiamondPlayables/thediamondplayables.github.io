RepliSherd lets you easily replicate pottery sherds!
This saves you time that would otherwise be spent on going to the same structure just to get the chance of getting the same sherd again.

This also lets you have tons of Sherds for builds or minigames!
In order to replicate it though, you still need to get the Sherd you want for the first time.

I just don't want to revisit the same areas just to get more of the same sherd.
