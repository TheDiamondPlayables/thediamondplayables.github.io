(This update was dropped 3 days earlier for Iron Patrons)

I've reworked how BlockSeat works with a better approach!
If you have any questions about this, let me know about it on Discord. Have fun!

Additions:

  • The cushion is back, lads!
    • If you sit with nothing on (even in your offhand), you'll now immediately make a new cushion!
    • Cushion placement differs depends on where you're sitting in.
  • Crouching in the edge of blocks now places cushions in a more accurate height!
    • I got quite a few iterations on this before, but none of them were efficient enough for shipping.
    • Finally got a pretty good solution though!
  • New sitting particles!

Changes:

  • Reworked sitting mechanics!
    • If you'd have to crouch alot for non-sitting reasons, BlockSeat shouldn't affect your game.
    • This was the main goal for BlockSeat's design, but past iterations wasn't that great.
    • The new iteration works much more efficiently too, so that's a bonus.
  • Cushion placement now works well on fences!
  • Cushion placement now also works well on block edges!
  • Fixed a bug causing players to clip through the floor when if they sit while crouched.
    • Sorry for the wait on this patch.
    • I knew I had to rework the entire system anyway so I figured I'd put this in with that as well.
    • Just didn't expected it to take this long, ya know?

New sitting mechanics:

  • Simply crouch with nothing in your inventory.
    • A cushion will appear that lets you sit on it, like you do in past versions.
    • Or you can hit the seat to remove it, if you don't want to sit right now.
    • I've basically scrapped the items filter at this point. Works much better without one is what I find.

Note: The cushion won't spawn again until you've started crouching again.
This should make it a lot less annoying for cases where you'd need to use crouch for non-sitting reasons.
