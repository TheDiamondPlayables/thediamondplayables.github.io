CropBooster makes Composters boost surrounding crops with its Compost!
This is an optional Composter upgrade that doesn't affect the vanilla Composter.

This means you can:

  • Upgrade the Composter to make it boost crops around itself,
  • Or keep the Composter if you only need its vanilla use.

The CropBooster also supports hopper feeding for more automation, too!
