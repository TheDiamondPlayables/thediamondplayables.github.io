Changes:

  • Is now designed for 1.21.6! (Ported from Version 21e1.)
  • Added new heads: Ghastling, Happy Ghast
    • Happy Ghasts have a 35% chance of dropping the Ghastling head instead of it's head.
      • This is mainly to avoid making you kill babies in order to get Ghastling heads.
    • If the Happy Ghast was killed with fire and a head would've dropped, Dried Ghast is dropped instead.
  • Added 9 new sounds:
    • Ghastling (4): All available sounds.
    • Happy Ghast (5): All except Equip, Unequip and Riding.
      • The first 2 are strictly Harness sounds, and the last one has no sound attached.
  • Updated the Ghast head texture to match the new version.
    • The old one is now known as the Old Ghast Head and has a 25% chance to replace the Ghast Head drop.
  • Changed background and icon to match current game drop's theme.
