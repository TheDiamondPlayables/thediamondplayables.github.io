(Also known as the Head Haven mega-update)

Additions:

  • Added 3104 new head variants:
    • Armadillo Head
    • Wolf Heads: Woods, Ashen, Black, Chestnut, Rusty, Spotted, Striped, Snowy.
      • This includes their corresponding Angry and A special variant that contains dog-specific sounds. Has a chance to replace the regular Wolf Head.Dog variants.
    • Breeze Head
    • Bogged Skull, Snipped Bogged Skull
    • Sheared Sheep Head
    • Noisy variants for Creeper Head, Wither Skeleton Skull, and Dragon Head.
      • These variants exist to support more note block sounds for these mobs.
      • One doesn't simply fit extra sounds into Vanilla heads ...
  • Added 33 new head sounds:
    • Armadillo Head (8): All sounds except:
      • Landing, Stepping - Not specific enough to armadillos.
      • Peeking, Unroll Peek - Is a sequence of sounds, not really a sound.
      • Scute Brush - Same sound as Scute Shed.
    • Parrot Head (2): Breeze, Bogged imitation sounds
    • Bogged Head (3): All bogged sounds, These sounds were not distinct enough.except Step and Shear.
    • Breeze Head (12): All breeze sounds, except This sound was way too quiet.Whirl
    • Ender Dragon (4): All dragon sounds, except This was the same as Growl, just different subtitle.Roar and This is a long sequence of sounds, not really a sound.Death.
    • Wither Skeleton (4): All Wither Skeleton sounds.
  • You can now transfer sounds between mob heads!
    • To do this, get the head's sound by breaking it with a Brush, set it in your offhand, then brush another head with your mainhand to copy the head's sound!
    • For this to work, the 2 heads must share the same set of sounds.
    • Won't work on Vanilla heads - their sounds can't be tweaked anyway.
  • New advancement: Something to Brush with ...
    • It's a tutorial for the new copy head sounds mechanic.

Changes:

  • Head texture tweaks:
    • The existing Wolf heads: Now looks more consistent with the new variants.
      • They also got a new Pale prefix to match the other variants.
    • Farmer Villager and Zombie Villager heads:
      • I tweaked these to give its signature hat some depth, while also try to make it look more like a hat.
    • Sheep heads:
      • The face is now more visible when seen from the front, like it does for the Sheep.
  • Also known as drop rate tweaks.Player Kill tweaks:
    • The old head variants are now 3x less likely to show up compared to the head variants you'd normally expect.
      • Mainly because I want the head you expect a mob to drop is most likely the one you'll get first.
      • I also want the old variants to reflect how tricky it is to get access to ancient game knowledge.
    • Tripled the average amount of Cod and Salmon kills needed to get a head.
      • These respawn pretty quickly, and often in schools, so it was much easier to get these than I initially thought they would.
    • Lowered Tropical Fish head drop rate by 2.5x.
      • Same logic with Cod/Salmon heads, but we got a lot more variants for these things.
    • Raised the amount of kills needed to get a Pufferfish head by 1.45x.
      • These don't show up in schools like the others, but getting one was still considered to be a bit too easy.
    • Dropped Noisy Head variants' replacement rate from 50% to 25%.
      • For Wither Skeletons, this is 2.5% due to how valuable it's vanilla counterpart is.
    • From 2.1% to 1.8%.Lowered Slime Head rates due to Potion of Oozing.
    • From 3.3% to 2.5%.Lowered Silverfish Head rates due to Potion of Infestation.
  • Custom Note Block Sound tweaks:
    • Heads when brushed now causes block updates, which can be paired with existing Redstone mechanics!
    • Now plays the sound the head just switched to if a Note Block is below the head.
      • If not, it'll use a custom sound cue.
      • This lets you know brushing has worked in cases where particles are too far to be rendered in.
    • Now cycles through head sounds when brushed continuously!
      • This lets you cycle through to your desired sound really quickly, which saves a lot of time.
      • It also lets you quickly go through all the possible sounds you just got from that new head of yours.
    • Raised the chances to get regular Parrot sounds.
      • Parrot sounds should always be as likely to show up as the mob imitation sounds.
    • Now adapts to your current block interaction range!
    • Effect colors now have randomization for more variety!
  • The old Tropical Fish Head is now called Archaic Tropical Fish Head.
    • For Appears 90% of the time, has a special name when put on a Bucket.common Tropical Fishes: This has a 10% chance to replace your expected head variant as the drop.
    • It's a good reminder of how annoyed I was having to settle for only one tropical fish head at the time.
  • Baby Zombie Villagers no longer drop profession-based heads.
    • From the server's view: Zombie Villagers are given random professions when they spawned in, but no exception was made for the babies, even though they're not supposed to have jobs in this universe.
    • Unless made visible via a Resource Pack, you normally won't be able to see it, so I tweaked it to be more consistent to what you're actually seeing.
  • Head sources now reflect how the head dropped, not how the mob died.
    • This means if the drop case is not allowed, it's head source won't be available.
  • Fixes and improvements:
    • Fixed "Not Behind" advancement not counting if you picked up a Creeper/Piglin Head.
    • Fixed a bug causing Wither Skeletons to drop only coal instead of coal and bones.
    • Improved Wither Boss's head drop processing performance.
  • Wither Heads now drop based on Player Kill chance if Wither Kill is the only active setting, instead of being a guaranteed drop.
  • Wither Skeleton Skulls now drop based on Player Kill chance if no settings are active, or Wither Kill is the only active setting.
    • In previous versions, you get Wither Skeleton Skulls via Charged Creepers if Wither Kill the only head case available, but it's not really that obvious.
    • If no settings are active, it's basically vanilla, so Wither Skeleton Skulls should still drop because of that.
  • Player Heads' Cause of Death rework:
    • Now shows quite a bit more detail on how you died.
      • Has the following format: <type> <source> <details, if any>
      • Can also be Shenanigans if it can't be detected.
    • Death type can be one of the following:
      • Environment kills: Uses a tree icon.
      • Player kills: Uses a controller icon.
      • Non player kills: Uses a sword icon.
    • Death source:
      • If environment kill: Name of the death environment.
        • Supported environments: Cactus, Crammed, Drowned, Exploded, Fireballed, Fireworks, Kinetic Energy, Frozen, On Magma, Suffocated, In Lava, Void, Out of Border, Starved, Withered, On Fire, Fall Damage, Lightning Struck, Falling Block, Sweet Berry Bush
      • If player/non player kill: Name of killer.
    • Death details:
      • For direct kills, or if not identified: No details are provided.
      • For indirect kills: Shows the kill method's name.
        • Supported methods: Sonic Boom, Spat, Thorns, Magic, Arrow, Trident, Wither Skull, Wind Charge, Fireball, Explosion
    • This should make storing your death history far more interesting, if you'll even have any.
  • Details:
    • Changed background to match current game drop's theme.
    • Changed Skeleton Shot drop case to Skeleton Kill.
      • Bogged and Wither Skeleton kills now count towards the Skeleton Kill head drop case.
    • Potion Kill has been renamed to Potion Damage.
    • Charged Creeper Kill and Creeper Kill have been renamed to Charged Creeper Blast and Creeper Blast respectively.
    • Demonic Panda Head has been renamed to Evil Panda Head.
      • Now has a 20% chance to replace Aggressive Panda Head (instead of 50%)
    • Shellless Shulker Head: Is now 2% + 1% per looting instead of the fixed 5%.
    • Pumpkin Mask's name has been removed.
    • Rewrote existing setting descriptions:
      • Now highlight the more important bits instead of notable subjects.
      • Reduced unnecessary differences between cases so you can read through them quicker.
  • Hold Ctrl, then press your Pick Block key.Ctrl-Picking now gives you the exact head as the one currently placed.
    • Head's sound variant will also be shown as if mined with a Brush.
    • Because of this, it's tooltip has been changed from "Brushed Sound" to "Current Sound".
  • Editing the head's name via the Anvil now saves the name when it's mined.
  • Toast and Rainbow Sheep Heads now drop as long as the name matches, regardless of formatting.
  • Decapitation's Head Drop Chances file has been tweaked:
    • Is now moved to the Interactive Help File.
    • Now shows you all the game's mobs and their head drop chances.
    • Also contains how many head variants you can expect to find from this mob.
      • Because we got over 3000 head variants now, showing each head's drop rates becomes impractical.
      • This approach also won't spoil all the head variants one can expect to find, which makes the whole hunting process much more interesting.

    Admin Settings:

    • The in-game settings system has now been reworked, and is now known as Admin Settings.
      • You can access these settings by typing /function tdp.config:decapitation in chat.
    • Added new Settings:
      • Brush Updates: If brushing heads causes block updates.
      • Player Head Drops: Enable/Disable Player Head drops.
    • Admin Book now saves your current page and loops around, which makes editing easier!
    • Added Setting Presets: Save and load your preferences from different worlds, or online!
      • This can be found in the Admin Book's Presets page.

    Update Notes:

    • If you're upgrading from pre-1.21:
      • Your existing settings won't be saved to this version because everything was wrecked when 1.20.5 attacked.
      • The internal head format has also been wrecked when 1.20.5 attacked, so that had to be reworked.
        • You can migrate heads from the old format to the new one by changing it's Note Block Sound.
        • Existing Note Block head sounds aren't affected, so your contraptions will still work just fine.

    Extras:

    • After 6 years of making datapacks, we finally have icons now!
    • Improved the look of info text messages.
    • Text files have been merged into one single Interactive Help File.
      • I have to learn 3 new languages to get all this to work as well as it did.
      • Not the best at it yet, but it works well enough for now.
    • Codebase now uses Scripter 4.
