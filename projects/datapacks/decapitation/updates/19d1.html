Important changes:

  • Is now designed for Minecraft 1.19.4.
    • It's not advised to run this in older versions, I won't be able to provide much support if you do.
      (Imagine having to do that for all older versions for all my datapacks, you'll see a scale issue)
  • Updating to the newest Decapitation will cause all its settings to be reset.
    • I'll work on a stable upgrade system in the future, for now it is what it is.

Gameplay Changes:

  • Players now always drop their heads on death, no matter the condition.
    • This has always been the case, but when I reworked the system for Scripter 3, I forgot to check for this small case, so it snuck by unnoticed. Whoops!

    • A quick note here: This head sound design won't be the final design in use for 1.20.
