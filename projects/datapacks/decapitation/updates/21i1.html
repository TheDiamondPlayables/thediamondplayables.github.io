Changes:

  • Is now designed for 1.21.9 and 1.21.10! (Ported from Version 21g1.)
  • Added 8 new heads for the Copper Golem!
    • These heads are more Pumpkin than they are Copper, so they won't act like Copper Blocks.
    • They're also not complete Pumpkin, so you can't use them to resurrect the Copper Golem.
  • Added new sounds for the Copper Golem heads!
  • Charged Creeper head source improvements:
    • Turning off Charged Creeper head drop case now works on mobs that drop vanilla heads!
    • Noisy variants of Vanilla Heads can now be obtained under the Charged Creeper source!
    • Charged Creepers now drop Charged Creeper Head if killed by a Charged Creeper!
    • I would've done these before, if Charged Creeper drops weren't hardcoded back then.
  • Changed background and icon to match current game drop's theme.

Developer's Comments:

  • I'd experimented with making the Copper Golem Heads Oxidizing normally, can be waxed and scraped.act more like Copper, but I found that what the change ultimately did is that it asks more processing power from the game in order to make what would otherwise be 8 distinctive heads feel like they're just 1 head.
  • So to preserve the heads' collectible value, these heads will be the sole exception when it comes to Copper stuff.
    • Think of the heads as being more Pumpkin than Copper - I mean, you made them with a Pumpkin, there still has to be some of it in them somewhere, right?
