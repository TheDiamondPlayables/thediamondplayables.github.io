Merchant is an improved Wandering Trader that offers fairer trades!
The Merchant also spawn in all 3 dimensions, offering dimension-specific trades from all over the world!

  • Merchant's offers reflect the items' rarity in the world.
  • This makes non-renewables renewable at a reasonable cost.

Merchant does not affect vanilla Wandering Traders' mechanics.
Just so you can compare between the two.

Honestly though, the Wandering Trader's deals are just bad.
